Relocation

Salary cost-of-living comparator

Enter your salary in city A and COL indices for A and B on the same scale (higher = more expensive). Equivalent salary in B = salary × (indexB / indexA).

Indices vary by source (Numbeo, C2ER, OECD, etc.). Use paired estimates from one methodology; mix sources at your own risk.
