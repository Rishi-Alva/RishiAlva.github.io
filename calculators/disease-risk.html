Screening literacy

Disease risk estimator

Two separate highly simplified point tallies inspired by public screening tools. They cannot diagnose disease or replace labs and exams.

Type 2 diabetes (simplified risk points)

Loosely based on common questionnaire factors (age, BMI, family history, activity, prior high glucose). For learning only.

Heart disease (very rough points)

Not Framingham or ASCVD. A toy sum of age, blood pressure band, smoking, and cholesterol awareness—use only to discuss prevention with a clinician.

If you have symptoms (chest pain, sudden weakness, thirst with weight loss, etc.), seek urgent care. Prevention planning belongs with your doctor.
