Fluids

Hydration needs

Rule-of-thumb: about 30–35 ml per kg body weight, plus a bump for higher activity or heat. Output is total fluids from drinks + watery foods, not a prescription.

Inputs

Heart, kidney, or electrolyte disorders often need fluid limits. Follow your care team if that applies to you.
