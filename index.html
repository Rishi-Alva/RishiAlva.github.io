Quick answers

What analytics work do you focus on?

Survey and operational data, metric design, cleaning and QA, analysis in R or Python when needed, and Tableau or Excel outputs people actually open. I also care about documentation so the work is repeatable.

How do you explain results to non-technical stakeholders?

I start with the decision, not the method. Plain language, a clear visual hierarchy, and a short “so what”: what moved, why it matters, and two or three options for what to do next.

What are you looking for next?

Roles where analytics supports strategy, especially at the intersection of health, data, and consulting-style problem framing. I value teams that care about both rigor and communication.
